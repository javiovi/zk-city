zkCity

A one-week immersive bootcamp in Buenos Aires designed to foster cross-disciplinary breakthroughs.

Contribute Apply now

The event will bring together 50 students from diverse backgrounds such as Mathematics, Computer Science and Engineering who will have the unique opportunity to learn from top-class cryptographers and engineers from established zero-knowledge projects.

With its in-person format, the event cultivates an environment of deep collaboration, the exchange of ideas, and insightful discussions.

The experience culminates in a hackathon, providing an opportunity for students to put their newfound knowledge into practice.

Introduction
to Cryptography

Learn what cryptography is, exploring intractable problems, Discrete Logarithm, RSA, Diffie-Hellman, digital signatures, key-exchange, hash functions, and Bitcoin.

Proof
Systems

Understand cryptocurrency transactions, proofs of knowledge: definition and intuition, zero-knowledge proofs, and practical examples like knowledge of discrete log and ZCash.

zkSNARKs

Dive into zkSNARKs with general recipes, polynomial interactive oracle proofs, KZG polynomial commitments, pairings, and the Algebraic Group Model (AGM).

Design

Explore SNARKs for specific relations, such as vector commitments and lookup arguments.

Beyond the Basics

Study different setups (trusted, updatable, transparent), NP problems with various arithmetizations (Plonk, R1CS, QAPs), and advanced concepts like STARKs and Groth16.
